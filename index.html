Whom Will You Serve?

“Now if it seems bad to you to serve Jehovah, choose for yourselves today whom you will serve… But as for me and my household, we will serve Jehovah.” (Joshua 24:15)

Read More

Your life’s most vital decision looms: will you serve Satan’s fleeting world or embrace Jehovah’s everlasting Kingdom? Joshua’s challenge rings true today: “Choose for yourselves today whom you will serve… But as for me and my household, we will serve Jehovah” (Joshua 24:15). Temporary relief or permanent solution? Only Jehovah’s truth leads to life. Why choose His Kingdom?

  • Satan’s World: Lies and Deception:
    This world’s systems—politics, religion, commerce—promise freedom but breed chaos, greed, and despair (1 John 5:19). False teachings, like the immortal soul or eternal torment, rooted in human tradition, contradict the Bible’s truth that the dead are unconscious (Ecclesiastes 9:5).

    • The whole world is lying in the power of the wicked one. — 1 John 5:19

  • Jehovah’s Kingdom: Truth and Honesty:
    Jehovah’s Word cuts through deception, revealing His Kingdom as a real government that will crush human rule and bring peace (Daniel 2:44). Unlike shifting human promises, Jehovah’s word is unbreakable; He cannot lie (Numbers 23:19).

    • God is not a man that he should lie… — Numbers 23:19

  • Temporary Relief vs. Permanent Solution:
    Satan’s world tempts with short-lived pleasures—wealth, status, indulgence—but they vanish (1 John 2:17). Jehovah’s Kingdom will transform earth into a paradise, erasing pain, sickness, and death forever (Revelation 21:4). Why chase shadows when Jehovah offers eternity?

    • God will wipe out every tear from their eyes, and death will be no more… — Revelation 21:4

  • Moral Standards That Endure:
    Satan’s world glorifies selfishness, immorality, and violence, degrading humanity (2 Timothy 3:1-5). Jehovah’s standards—love, integrity, purity—uplift lives and build lasting joy (Colossians 3:12-14). Living His way brings purpose now and prepares you for life without end.

    • Put on the new personality that was created according to God’s will in true righteousness and loyalty. — Ephesians 4:24

  • Your Choice Shapes Your Future:
    There’s no middle ground; indecision aligns with Satan’s world (Matthew 12:30). Choosing Jehovah means rejecting false teachings, studying His Word, and living His truth (John 4:23). This decision unlocks the path to everlasting life.

    • This means everlasting life, their taking in knowledge of you, the only true God… — John 17:3

Satan’s world is doomed to collapse; Jehovah’s Kingdom stands forever. Make the choice that secures your future—serve Jehovah and inherit life eternal!

The meek will possess the earth, and they will find exquisite delight in the abundance of peace. — Psalm 37:11

Your Choice, Your Life!

“I have set before you life and death, the blessing and the curse. Therefore choose life, that you and your offspring may live.” (Deuteronomy 30:19)

Read More

Your choice today determines your eternal future: will you cling to Satan’s fading world or embrace Jehovah’s promise of life? Deuteronomy’s urgent call resonates: “Choose life, that you and your offspring may live” (Deuteronomy 30:19). By choosing Jehovah, you reject deception, live His truth, and secure everlasting life. Why is this choice critical?

  • Satan’s World: A Path to Death:
    Under Satan’s influence, this world promotes lies—false religions, materialism, and moral decay—that lead to destruction (2 Corinthians 4:4). Teachings like the immortal soul or eternal torment deceive millions, but the Bible reveals the dead are unconscious (Ecclesiastes 9:10).

    • The god of this system of things has blinded the minds of the unbelievers… — 2 Corinthians 4:4

  • Jehovah’s Truth: The Way to Life:
    Jehovah’s Word exposes lies and reveals His Kingdom, a real government that will restore earth to paradise (Isaiah 9:7). His promises never fail, offering hope and certainty in an unstable world (Titus 1:2).

    • In the hope of the everlasting life that God, who cannot lie, promised… — Titus 1:2

  • Fleeting Pleasures vs. Eternal Joy:
    Satan’s world entices with temporary thrills—wealth, fame, indulgence—but they end in emptiness (1 John 2:17). Jehovah’s Kingdom brings lasting happiness, erasing sorrow, sickness, and death forever (Revelation 21:4).

    • God will wipe out every tear from their eyes, and death will be no more… — Revelation 21:4

  • Jehovah’s Standards: A Life of Purpose:
    This world’s selfishness and immorality degrade humanity (Romans 1:28-32). Jehovah’s principles—love, honesty, compassion—ennoble lives, giving meaning now and preparing you for eternity (Micah 6:8).

    • He has told you, O man, what is good. And what is Jehovah requiring of you? Only to exercise justice, to cherish loyalty, and to walk in modesty with your God. — Micah 6:8

  • Your Decision, Your Destiny:
    There’s no neutrality; indecision sides with Satan’s doomed world (Revelation 3:15-16). Choosing Jehovah means studying His Word, rejecting falsehoods, and living His way—your path to everlasting life (John 17:3).

    • This means everlasting life, their taking in knowledge of you, the only true God… — John 17:3

Satan’s world is crumbling; Jehovah’s Kingdom is forever. Your choice shapes your life—choose Jehovah and live eternally!

The righteous will possess the earth, and they will live forever on it. — Psalm 37:29
